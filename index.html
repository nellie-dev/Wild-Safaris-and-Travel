Welcome to Wild Safaris and Travel

Wild Safaris and travel is a Safari company based in Nairobi Kenya, and was established 12 years ago to help you fulfill your dreams of an African Safari Adventure. We allow our guests the freedom to choose how to spend their time here, ours is just guiding and advising! We have our own experienced tour staff, plus airport representatives, drivers, guides, camping crews and safari vehicle fleets. Our portfolio extends beyond the traditional safari with dynamic, innovative African adventures, active safaris, and incentive services.
